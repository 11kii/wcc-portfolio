Top-Viewed Wikipedia Articles of the Week (February 5 to 11, 2023)

Wikipedia is a vast online encyclopedia that provides access to information on a wide range of topics. The popularity of Wikipedia articles can change quickly, with some articles becoming highly sought after by readers. This list features the most popular Wikipedia articles of the week from February 5 to 11, 2023, based on the number of page views. These articles cover a diverse range of topics, from current events and popular culture to history and science, and provide a snapshot of what people were searching for and reading about during this specific week in time. The list offers a fascinating insight into the interests and concerns of the general public and provides a window into what was capturing people's attention during this particular time period.
